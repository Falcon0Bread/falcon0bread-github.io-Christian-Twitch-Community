Online Church

Every person should have the ability and chance to find their community. Some find it online, in person, or mixed. Twitch is able to offer that by not just connecting online but the views do have an impact on the streamer and the same for the streamer themselves. The viewers are able to connect with streamers in real time and the feeling of being noticed in a busy world amplifies. With the Christian community it’s ways of streaming and connection are different. Most streamers will not allow for random viewers to be in their private dms, but Christian Streamers do. This allows for a greater connection to form with viewers and the community is stronger because of that. Yes, we will not tolerate disrespect but we want to replicate Jesus, so we love as best we can. Jesus is able to do more than allow us to say encouraging things on stream. By praying together on stream, we are able to bear each other's burdens in real time. This makes people feel acknowledged and have a feeling of worth.
