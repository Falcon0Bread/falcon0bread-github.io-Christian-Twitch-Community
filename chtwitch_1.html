The Digital Kingdom

The Christian Twitch Community is very vast in size and there is a lot of care. Being a part of this community feels to me with peace and I feel a massive belonging in the space. Our goal is to share the Love of Jesus to everyone, every person needs to know who He is and what He can do. The community is a very fun and safe place to be, and every one does different things. I want the theme of the zine to really display the care and fun that the Christian Twitch Community has, and how we all want to have a great time and show people how amazing Jesus is. The cool thing is that you do not have to be Christian to be in our community, although we will openly talk about Jesus and the teachings that the Bible covers. There will be times when we disagree but in the end we have the love that Jesus has. If you do end up being disrespectful will warn you, and most of our audiences include children. Not every streamer is the same and everyone is at a different part with their journey with Jesus. Some do curse, but they will be working on reducing that, at the end we are all still people. We have moments when we fall but that does not make an excuse to be disrespectful, at the end of the day we answer to one person; that is Jesus. We are very excited to see what Jesus is doing not just on Twitch but everywhere in the world. The very interesting thing is that you can be a part of that experience, even though you might not believe in Jesus; our streams are always open! We also have really fun events so do your best not to miss out!!
