Cyber Sanctuary

Feeling peace during a stream should be another important thing, and most streamers are not peaceful. Popular streamers curse a lot or say obscene things, you will not find that in a majority of Christian streams. Christian streamers are human, mistakes are made but we are overall family friendly. There are communities on Twitch that do not feature terrible content, like drawing (you have to be careful sometimes), djing, song writing, Aquariums and zoos, and much more. There is a sense of mindfulness when it comes to a majority of streamers and streams. If a person is young, it would be wise to ask a parent or guardian if someone is okay to watch, and if not there are better options. The meaning of Christian is “little Christ” or “little light”.

Our goal on Twitch is to be the light on Twitch, and everything that I have previously stated revolves around this goal. What is seen on Twitch , one would think is how a person actually lives their life. The streamer has a character or persona that they put on, while others are the version that people want them to see. This duality can be difficult to navigate through, this is something I wrestle with at times. I personally feel that I can be myself when I am streaming, and people in my waking life only get bits and pieces of me. Through Jesus though I am able to be the person that I was made to be, and this is one of the main reasons why we stream. The real reason is to make our goal; that when we stream we stream for the person that does not know Jesus.
